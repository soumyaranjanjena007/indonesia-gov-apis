# EITI Indonesia — Extractives Transparency
**Organization:** EITI / Kementerian ESDM
**Portal:** https://eiti.org/indonesia | https://eiti.esdm.go.id
**API type:** ⚠️ CSV/XLSX reports

## Overview
Beneficial ownership in mining & oil/gas. Company-level revenue and payment data from extractive industries. Annual reports cover companies, licenses, production volumes.

## Gotchas
1. Annual reports available as downloads
2. Linked to AHU beneficial ownership database initiative
3. Covers mining, oil & gas revenue transparency
